Hello! I earned my BSc degree from the College of Ocean and Earth Science, Xiamen University. Now I am a dual degree Ph.D. student University of Delaware (Dr. Wei-Jun Cai) and Xiamen University (Dr. Xiao-Hai Yan; also at UD). I am interested in the coastal CO2 carbon cycle, extreme climate events, and satellite oceanography. My current research focuses on the Gulf of Mexico and Mid-Atlantic Bight air-sea CO2 fluxes.
