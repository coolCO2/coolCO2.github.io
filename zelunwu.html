Hello! I earned my BSc degree from the College of Ocean and Earth Science, Xiamen University. Now I am a Ph.D. student of Dr. Wei-Jun Cai at the University of Delaware. I am interested in the coastal CO2 carbon cycle, extreme climate events, and satellite oceanography. My current research focuses on the Gulf of Mexico and Mid-Atlantic Bight air-sea CO2 fluxes.
