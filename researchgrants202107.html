Research grant

bestowment

In summer 2021, C.O.O.L lab received two new federal research grants. The first is an award from NASA (with PI Dr. Yun Li) on remote sensing of CO₂ and understand its control mechanism in the Arctic Ocean. The second is an award from NSF’s Chemical Oceanography program (with co-PI Dr. Eliot Atekwana). The project title is: "Measurements of stable carbon isotopes (δ¹³C-DIC) on board GO-SHIP cruises in the South Atlantic Ocean to enhance our ability to quantify anthropogenic CO₂ uptake rates by the ocean.
