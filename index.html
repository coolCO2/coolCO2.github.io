Cai Co2 ocean
observation Laboratory
(C.O.O.L.)

The C.O.O.L. (CO2L) group, led by Professor Wei-Jun Cai, specializes in the marine and estuarine CO2-carbonate cycle and the anthropogenic impacts on the system. The group resides at the University of Delaware at the College of Earth, Ocean, and Environment
within the School of Marine Science and Policy.
